Beach

Planning a day at the beach is easy with our guide to Cape Cod’s best beaches. From rules and parking to passes and travel tips, you’ll find everything you need to enjoy a safe and relaxing visit

Beach Rules

  • Swim in designated areas only
  • No littering — use trash and recycling bins
  • Pets may be restricted on certain beaches or require leashes
  • Fires and alcohol are typically prohibited
  • Always follow posted signs and local regulations

Parking and Pricing

  • Most beaches have designated parking areas. Fees and pass requirements vary by location and season
  • Daily passes: Required at many beaches during peak season
  • Seasonal passes: Provide unlimited access for a set period
  • Payment options: Available online or at local kiosks

Travel Tips

      Weather: Check forecasts before visiting. Summer is busiest, while spring and fall are quieter.
      Transportation: Beaches are accessible by car, bike, and public transportation. Plan ahead for parking and traffic.
      Local Resources: Visit town websites or visitor centers for updated hours, regulations, and events.

Plan Your Beach Visit

Review each beach’s access requirements and parking options in advance. Purchase passes or tickets online to ensure entry, and check for any seasonal or special regulations that may affect your visit.
