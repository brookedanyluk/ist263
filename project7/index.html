Suggested Itineraries

All Itinerary Details

Itinerary 1: Classic Cape Cod

Morning

  • Breakfast in Hyannis (try a local café or bakery)
  • Walk along Hyannis Harbor or Main Street shops

Afternoon

  • Drive to Nauset Beach (Eastham) or Craigville Beach
  • Lunch nearby (casual seafood or a beachside shack)

Evening

  • Dinner in Chatham
  • Stroll around Chatham Lighthouse at sunset

Itinerary 2: Active & Outdoorsy Cape

Morning

  • Rent bikes and ride the Cape Cod Rail Trail
  • Stop in Brewster for coffee or a quick snack

Afternoon

  • Visit the Cape Cod National Seashore
  • Explore Coast Guard Beach or Marconi Beach

Evening

  • Casual dinner in Wellfleet
  • Watch the sunset over Wellfleet Bay

Itinerary 3: Arts, Food, & Towns

Morning

  • Breakfast in Provincetown
  • Walk Commercial Street (galleries, shops, and cafés)

Afternoon

  • Visit the Pilgrim Monument
  • Lunch with ocean views
  • Ferry or drive to Truro for beaches or vineyards

Evening

  • Dinner in Provincetown
  • Optional live music or a sunset sail
